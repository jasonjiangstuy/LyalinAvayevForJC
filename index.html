Ad Populum

For the People

At the heart of the social contract is the belief that elected officials represent their voters -- that those voted into office will uphold the promises they made and work to improve the lives of those they made the promises to. That's why the Lyalin-Abayev ticket chose Ad Populum to represent itself: because we're running for, and will work to help, YOU -- the voters and the people.
