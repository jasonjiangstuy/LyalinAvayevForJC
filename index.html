Accountability, Novelty, Diversity
Accountability As elected leaders, we're here to represent you. To do that best, we want to hear your comments, critiques, suggestions, complaints, and everything in between; your thoughts can tell us what we're doing wrong, and what we're doing right. For that reason, accountability is important to us: it's our way of telling you that we're listening and we care.

Accountability -- For the people. 		Novelty Our role as co-presidents is to make your lives easier -- to constantly help ease and improve the struggle that is remote/hybrid learning. To do that, we need to innovate; we need to constantly be coming up with new resources to provide, new technologies to develop, and new ways to reach out and communicate with the junior class. For that reason, novelty is important to us: we hope our ability to foster new ideas can allow us to provide you with the best experience possible.

Novelty -- For the people. 		Diversity The Junior Caucus is exactly that: a body that is meant to listen to and represent ALL juniors. One of the core tenets of our platform is our willingness to listen to and represent all the people. It is our promise to you that every junior feel included in the discussion with us as presidents.

Diversity -- For the people.
