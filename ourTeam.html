Daniel Lyalin, Co-Presidential Candidate

Hey Juniors!

My name is Daniel Lyalin and I am the co-presidential candidate for the DNA campaign. As a freshmen and sophomore, I’ve been active in a wide variety of leadership roles, working behind the scenes to keep many of Stuy’s clubs and extracurriculars running smoothly. You may know me as a Red Cross board member, a position from which I’ve worked to organize many of the volunteer events you’ve come to expect from New York City’s biggest Red Cross club; to that end, I’ve also accrued 60+ volunteer hours helping out around the city! As the Vice President and an active member of Ethics Forum, I’ve striven to expose Stuyvesant students to moral philosophy both through direct student outreach and through the development of Ethical Inquiries (and Where to Find Them), the clubs biannual magazine; as an active member of the club since freshmen year, I’ve seen myself grow as a person, developing a stronger moral compass and sense of direction. As a tutor for the Writing Center, I’ve aided many of my fellow juniors throughout the writing and editing process, discovering meanwhile how fulfilling tutoring can be.

When I’m not volunteering or drafting ethics arguments, you can find me playing tennis with the boys varsity team over at Pier 40, or watching some standup comedy on Netflix (I love John Mulaney and James Acaster). I’m also a lowkey music fan, and love to collect vinyl, especially NYC jazz rap.

Having known Emmanuel Abayev since middle school, I can say with confidence that he is motivated and capable of enacting real change; his commitment to diversity and accountability, not to mention his knack for innovation, is why I choose to run with him for Junior Caucus not as President and Vice President, but as Co-Presidents -- as partners who know and trust one another. I hope that the junior class will come to know and trust us as well should we be elected.

Yours Truly,
Daniel Lyalin



Emmanuel Abayev, Co-Presidential Candidate

Hi Juniors!

I’m Emmanuel, and I’m running for Junior Caucus Co-President alongside Daniel Lyalin! Over the past two years, I have watched the Student Union and my own Freshman and Sophomore Caucuses from afar, and I can confidently say that there is room for improvement, and we are here to bring it to you!

Inside Stuy, I’ll often be spending my time in the Writing Center or StuyLaunch, while I’ve been spending time outside of school as a senior writer for Prep Hoops NY, working on research projects with Professors from USC and Rutgers, or spending time in an internship with Repute PR. In my free time, I like to hit the fairway, or, more realistically, sit on the couch watching some quality Netflix.

When I decided to run, picking a running mate was an absolute no-brainer. I have known Daniel Lyalin since middle school, and I have no doubt that his previous contribution to society, as well as his hardworking spirit would be perfect for this office. His devotion to diversity, novelty, and accountability make him perfect for Junior Caucus, and I hope you all can see the same.

After we all experienced four months of school-from-home, our Lyalin-Abayev ticket specializes in making the remote/blended learning experience more efficient, enjoyable, and wholesome. Our platform is built on diversity, novelty, and accountability, the core principles of any quality platform, and it’s these principles that let us guarantee a meaningful junior year to each and every junior. I strongly urge you to raise your glass to a great junior year, and to invest your vote in the Lyalin-Abayev ticket!

Yours Truly,
Emmanuel Abayev
