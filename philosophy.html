Our Philosophy

We believe in a Junior Caucus that actively represents the best interests of the student body in everything it does. Our boundless enthusiasm to institute reform and make sure that all members of the Junior community have an equal voice stems from a deep-seated desire for unity. Unity has often been misrepresented as homogeny and agreement, and our platform prioritizes anything but that. To us, unity is about diversity, novelty, and accountability. We make a commitment to clarity of records and speech, transmitting reports, finances, and decisions directly to the student body. We seek to provide a breath of fresh air to our fellow peers, proposing innovative programs and new solutions to aid juniors in the unfamiliar situations of blended and remote learning. Our administration would work to celebrate and encourage the diversity of thought and background that we see in the Stuyvesant community. In promoting unity between all members of our cohort, we hope to build bridges between the decisions made by the Junior Caucus and the wonderfully diverse Junior student body.

Yours Truly,
